Account Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your account and associated data, please follow the steps below:

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the app and navigate to the settings menu.
  2. Select "Delete Account."
  3. Confirm your decision by entering your password.
  4. Your request will be processed, and you will receive a confirmation email.

Data Deletion Policy

Upon account deletion, the following data will be removed:

  • User profile data
  • Uploaded content
  • Preferences and settings

Data retained includes:

  • Transaction history for legal compliance (retained for 30 days)
